    About HQ Command Centre v2

    Unified dashboard merging Pantry Command (inventory, shopping, menu planning) with Tight Ship (personal finance tracking).

    Shopping list integrates with Finance — log checked purchases directly as spend transactions.

    Pantry data syncs via Firebase Firestore (cloud + offline). Finance data stored in localStorage (device-only).

    HQ
    Welcome to HQ
    Your unified household dashboard. Track your pantry, organize shopping, plan meals, and manage your personal finances all in one place.
    01. Inventory
    Keep an eye on what you have. Use the 🎤 Voice Usage button to quickly say "I used 2 eggs" and the app will automatically deduct it.
    02. Shopping
    Hit Sync Low-Stock to instantly build your grocery list. When you buy items, log the total to send it directly to your Finance ledger.
    03. Menu Planner
    Paste messy recipes into Smart Paste. The app will organize the text and cross-check your inventory to flag any missing items.
    04. Tight Ship
    Log your income, track expenses, and manage upcoming bills. Financial data is kept strictly on your device for maximum privacy.
    ⚠️

    Wipe Finance Data?

    This permanently deletes all transactions from this device. This cannot be undone.
